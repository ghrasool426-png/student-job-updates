SSC GD Final Result 2025 Declared: 50,047 Candidates Selected

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has officially announced the SSC GD Final Result 2025 for the recruitment of Constable (General Duty). Candidates who appeared in the examination can now check the final selection status. As per the official write-up, a total of 50,047 candidates have been selected for appointment in various Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs).

Forces Included Under SSC GD Recruitment

Selected candidates will be appointed in the following forces:

  • Border Security Force (BSF)
  • Central Industrial Security Force (CISF)
  • Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF)
  • Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB)
  • Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP)
  • Assam Rifles (AR)
  • Secretariat Security Force (SSF)
  • Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB)
