Indian Navy 10+2 B.Tech Cadet Entry – July 2026

Complete Guide: Eligibility, Selection Process, Salary, Career Scope & Online Application

Indian Navy has invited online applications for the prestigious 10+2 (B.Tech) Cadet Entry Scheme – July 2026 Batch. This entry allows young aspirants to join the Indian Navy as Permanent Commission Officers while pursuing a fully sponsored B.Tech degree from the Indian Naval Academy.

Unlike regular engineering colleges, this scheme offers discipline, leadership training, technical education, and a guaranteed officer-level career after completion of training. The last date for online application has been extended till 23 January 2026, giving candidates additional time to apply.

Why this entry is special?
• No tuition fee
• Monthly stipend during training
• Permanent Commission as an Officer
• B.Tech Degree + Defence Career

Important Dates

  • Online Application Start: January 2026
  • Last Date to Apply: 23 January 2026 (Extended)
  • SSB Interviews: April – June 2026
  • Course Commencement: July 2026

Vacancy & Training Details

  • Total Vacancies: 44
  • Entry Type: 10+2 (B.Tech) Cadet Entry Scheme
  • Branches: Executive & Technical
  • Training Academy: Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Kerala
  • Training Duration: 4 Years

Educational Eligibility

Candidates must have completed their 10+2 education with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from a recognized board.

  • Minimum 70% marks in PCM
  • Minimum 50% marks in English (Class 10 or 12)
  • Appeared in JEE (Main) 2025

Age Limit Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 17 Years
  • Maximum Age: 19½ Years
  • Date of Birth Range: 02 January 2007 to 01 July 2009

Selection Process Explained

The selection process for Indian Navy B.Tech Cadet Entry is transparent and merit-based.

  • Shortlisting based on JEE (Main) All India Rank
  • Service Selection Board (SSB) Interview
  • Medical Examination
  • Final Merit List & Joining Letter

Training, Salary & Benefits

During Training

  • Monthly stipend as per Navy rules
  • Free accommodation, uniform & medical facilities

After Commission

  • Rank: Sub Lieutenant
  • Pay Level: Level 10 (₹56,100 basic pay)
  • Additional allowances: DA, HRA/Ship Allowance, TA

Career Growth in Indian Navy

After commissioning, officers get multiple opportunities for career growth, higher education, and specialization.

  • Promotions up to Admiral rank
  • Postgraduate & foreign training opportunities
  • Job security & lifelong pension benefits

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is there any application fee?

No. Indian Navy does not charge any application fee.

Is JEE Main mandatory?

Yes. Shortlisting is strictly based on JEE (Main) 2025 rank.

Is this entry for boys only?

This entry is currently open for male candidates as per official notification.

Is B.Tech degree valid outside Navy?

Yes. The degree is recognized and valid for higher studies and civilian careers.
