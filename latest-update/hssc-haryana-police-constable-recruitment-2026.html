HSSC Haryana Police Constable Recruitment 2026 – Apply Online for 5500 Posts

Post Date: 20 January 2026

The Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released an official notification for the recruitment of Haryana Police Constable (General Duty) posts. A total of 5500 vacancies are available for Male, Female and GRP Constable posts. Eligible candidates can submit their online applications from 11 January 2026 to 25 January 2026.
